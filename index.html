About me [CV]

I'm a Ph.D. student at the Chinese University of Hong Kong (Shenzhen) , advised by Prof. Xiaoguang Han at GAP Lab. Previously, I obtained my M.Sc. at the University of California, Irvine and B.Eng. at Xidian University. I've been very grateful to work at CVMI Lab advised by Prof. Xiaojuan Qi and SIAT-MMLab supervised by Prof. Yu Qiao .

My current research goal lies on designing intelligent algorithms to make the machine better understand and perceive individual 3D objects and complex 3D scenes.
